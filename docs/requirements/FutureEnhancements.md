# Future Enhancements

## Business Description
This section captures potential future capabilities that may be introduced as the solution evolves beyond the initial phase.

## Future Enhancements

### FE-001: Expanded Integration Sources
- **Description:** Support additional mailbox providers, log repositories, and automation systems.
- **Acceptance Criteria:** The requirement framework can accommodate new connectors without major redesign.
- **Priority:** Medium
- **Dependencies:** Architecture evolution
- **Future Notes:** Prioritize based on business demand.

### FE-002: Advanced AI Orchestration
- **Description:** Add richer AI-assisted reasoning and decision-support workflows.
- **Acceptance Criteria:** The solution can support more advanced prompt structures and response handling.
- **Priority:** Medium
- **Dependencies:** AI integration strategy
- **Future Notes:** Evaluate governance and compliance requirements.

### FE-003: Enhanced Reporting and Dashboarding
- **Description:** Provide richer operational reporting and lifecycle visibility for support teams.
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Reporting needs can be addressed without redesigning the core workflow.
- **Priority:** Medium
- **Dependencies:** Reporting architecture and data retention
- **Future Notes:** Prioritize based on stakeholder value.
