# Risks

## Business Description
This section identifies delivery and operational risks associated with the solution requirements and scope.

## Risks

### RK-001: Requirement Ambiguity
- **Description:** Unclear business expectations may cause inaccurate implementation planning.
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Requirements remain explicit and reviewable.
- **Priority:** High
- **Dependencies:** Stakeholder alignment
- **Future Notes:** Requirements should be reviewed regularly.

### RK-002: Dependency on External Systems
- **Description:** The solution depends on external systems and integrations that may evolve or fail.
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Dependency impacts are understood and documented.
- **Priority:** High
- **Dependencies:** External system readiness
- **Future Notes:** Integration contracts should be maintained.

### RK-003: Data Quality Variability
- **Description:** Incoming email content and logs may vary in quality and structure.
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Requirements account for variability in content quality.
- **Priority:** Medium
- **Dependencies:** Input handling strategy
- **Future Notes:** Validation rules may be strengthened over time.
