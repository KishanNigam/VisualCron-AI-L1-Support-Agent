# Coding Standards

## Purpose
This document defines the expected engineering standards for future implementation work in the solution.

## Scope
The standards apply to future development, review, and governance activities across the solution lifecycle.

## Design Decisions
- Favor readability, consistency, and maintainability over premature optimization.
- Establish conventions that support collaboration across a distributed engineering team.
- Keep standards aligned with enterprise SDLC and agile delivery expectations.

## Future Considerations
- Introduce language-specific style guidance once implementation begins.
- Add review checklists and quality gates as the team expands.
- Align standards with CI/CD and automated validation practices.

## Risks
- Inconsistent coding practices may increase maintenance overhead.
- Poor standards can make code reviews less effective.
- Lack of guidance can lead to divergence across contributors.

## Best Practices
- Keep standards explicit and lightweight.
- Make review expectations clear and consistent.
- Update standards as the engineering team matures.
